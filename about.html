Our Story

Built by Tech People,
For Tech People.

FixHussle was founded with one mission: to make high-quality tech repair accessible, transparent, and fast — no more shady repair shops or inflated bills.

Who We Are

Bengaluru's Most Trusted Tech Repair Studio

Founded in 2025, FixHussle started as a small two-person workshop in Koramangala with a single goal: fix every device right, the first time. We grew by earning trust — one repair at a time.

Today, our team of 5 certified technicians handles everything from cracked iPhone screens to full enterprise network deployments for startups and offices across the city.

Every repair is backed by our 90-day warranty, free diagnostic policy, and genuine parts commitment. No compromise — ever.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide every customer with an honest, fast, and premium repair experience — supported by skilled technicians, genuine parts, and transparent pricing with zero hidden costs.

We believe technology should empower people, and a broken device shouldn't slow anyone down. That's why we focus on speed without sacrificing quality.

🔭

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted independent tech service brand — known for its integrity, craftsmanship, and the relationships we build with every customer we serve.

We're building toward a network of FixHussle studios across India's major cities, maintaining the same boutique quality and personal service that defines us today.
