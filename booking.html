FAQs

Common Questions

How long does a repair take?

Most mobile and laptop repairs are completed within 2–4 hours. PC builds and network setups are quoted individually but typically done within the same business day.

What if you can't fix it?

We follow a strict no fix, no fee policy. If we can't repair your device, you pay nothing — including the diagnostic fee.

Do you offer home pickup?

Yes! For an additional ₹100–200 (based on distance), we offer doorstep pickup and delivery within Bengaluru.

Are the parts genuine?

Absolutely. We use OEM-certified or brand-approved parts for all repairs. We'll never use counterfeit components without informing you first.
